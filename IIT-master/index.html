I might not of been learning anything specifically but watching Ido edit a page of C# was something I had never seen before. I believe that was the moment I had decided information technology was my path.

Project Idea

My IT project idea would be to develop an automatic chatbot that aims to assist and guide those towards a path of fitness. Similar to online guides, books, videos, etc on fitness this chatbot would encompass all of those into a simple and discreet chat bot. Taking the form of a mobile application and/or an accessible website, this chatbot aims to take those who are too intimidated by the vast world of fitness and simplify their needs.

My motivation for this project comes from a personal experience. When I began my fitness journey I was fortunate to have a father that takes fitness very seriously and was more than obliged to help me along every step of my journey. He was there to show me what exercises I could be doing at the gym, what foods I should be eating to supplement my training, as well as breaking down the scientific and practical reasons for everything in between. Fast-forward a year and my roommate wanted to get in shape. He told me this at the start of 2019 and didn’t approach me for help until the end of the year. After I got him started on a simple workout and eating plan he opened up about how intimidating it was for him to get started on his own. He explained how the over abundance of information on the internet was scaring him getting started because he was not sure what would work. Additionally he expressed his embarrassment for coming to me for help, as he assumed it would be easy to figure out what to do. Bringing this to my attention did make me reflect how fortunate I was to have my father's guidance, rather than getting lost in research over keto vs paleo, intermittent fasting vs counting macros, compound lifts vs isolated lifts, etc. If I could condense all my fathers knowledge and counselling into a chatbot then getting started on your fitness journey should be easy and positive for anyone.

I would want my chatbot to be personalised for each user. I understand everyone's fitness journey absolutely does not start with a scrawny teenage boy looking to bulk up and hop on the gain train. This is why I would press that the chatbot opens the conversation with a simple question regarding the person's goals. This could be as simple as ‘Are you looking to lose or gain weight?’ From here the chatbot can start asking questions about the individuals circumstances (age/weight/height, how often they would be able to train, dietary restrictions). Once all initial questions can be sufficiently answered the chatbot can start developing personalised programs for our users. These programs are to include everything the user may need along their journey, including video tutorials and how to exercise, not just specific exercise movements such as how to squat but everything including how to warm up, gym etiquette, what to expect after workouts, etc. Dietary guides that suggest more than just a macro count, but meal recommendations, eating windows, reminders and importantly I would want the chatbot to be stern like my father was when I was slacking on my diet. The app is designed for beginners however I want the chatbot to be responsive to requests and well. An example of this could be a user asking “I can’t make it to the gym for legs today, what can I do at home?” or “My knee has been giving me hell, what leg exercises do not strain the knee joints?” (I understand the crude wording would probably not work with most chatbots, purely for example). The initial concept could be completed with a simple rule-based chat bot however if it is successful then upgrading to a hybrid that includes NPL (natural language processing), this would allow the bot to converse more naturally with its users. I believe with successful implementation of such a feature would allow the chatbot to expand beyond exercise and dietary needs into the general field of health. Potentially allowing the chatbot to assist users with all sorts of health related inquiries such as specific vitamin/nutrient needs. Proficient programming knowledge and skill would be required to develop such a chatbot. Python can be used to program chatbots as well as introduce neural networks to support natural language processing. If this project was successful I believe a path of fitness would become second nature to thousands more. Improving societies life spans, happiness, productivity and confidence.
